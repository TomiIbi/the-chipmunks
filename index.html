Below: Our Story

One particularly harsh winter, there was a nut shortage in the forest and their mother, Vinnie, took off to look for food.

In her absence, their tree was cut down and carted off to grace the entrance lobby of a city office building, with them in it.

They then hitch a ride in a basket of pastries carried by Dave Seville and wind up in his home- where they wreak havoc, cause uproar in his kitchen and get thrown out.
They eventually show Dave how well they can sing and become celebrities as a result.
